## Dashboard Overview

### 1. Sales Analysis Dashboard
**Regional Performance Breakdown**
- Sales by region with segment drill-down capability
- Identify high-performing and underperforming regions
- Track sales velocity by product category
- Category preferences by customer segment
- Monthly and seasonal trends

### 2. Profit Analysis Dashboard
**Profitability Insights**
- Profit by region and category with margin analysis
- Profitability trends across 2014-2017
- Discount impact assessmentâ€”correlate discount depth with profit levels
- Regional profitability hierarchy
- Identify margin-erosion categories

**Key Insight**: Profit trends reveal which categories and regions are truly valuable vs. high-volume but low-margin.

### 3. Year Over Year Dashboard
**Trend Identification & Growth Tracking**
- Year-over-year sales and profit growth rates
- Identify accelerating and decelerating categories
- Dynamic year filtering (2014-2017) for quick pivots
- Seasonal patterns and consistent vs. volatile growth
- Early warning indicators for declining trends

**Key Performance Indicators** at a glance

## Data Foundation

- **Dataset**: Sample - Superstore
- **Transaction Volume**: 9,995+ orders
- **Time Period**: 2014-2017
- **Key Tables**: 
  - **Orders** (9,995 records): Sales, profit, discount, quantity, customer details
  - **People** (5 records): Manager-to-region mapping for accountability
  - **Returns** (297 records): Return tracking linked to original transactions

**Available Dimensions**:
- Geography: Region, State, City, Postal Code
- Business: Segment (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office), Category, Sub-Category
- Products: Product ID, Product Name
- Temporal: Order Date, Ship Date (2014-2017)
